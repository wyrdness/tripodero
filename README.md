## Overview

The Tripodero is a fearsome critter with three telescopic legs and a blowgun-like snout that shoots clay pellets to stun prey. Found near clay banks in the American Southwest, it represents the absurd creativity of lumberjack folklore.

**Hunting Method**: Stands on tripod legs, loads clay in hollow snout, shoots pellets with compressed air to stun prey.

---

## Characteristics

- **Three legs**: Hollow, telescopic, tripod stance
- **Blowgun snout**: Hollow snout shoots clay pellets
- **Habitat**: Clay banks, southwestern regions
- **Diet**: Prey stunned by clay bombardment

---

## Status: Tall Tale

Pure absurdist humor, never meant to be believed.
